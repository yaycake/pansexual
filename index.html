About

52 pages of saxophone-playing vaginas, phallic delights, and other portals to carnal treasures, the coloring book aims to celebrate sexual expression, positivity, and the promotion of sexual health education. Recommended for ages 18+; user discretion is advised.
Yaycake is an artist who enjoys drawing, collaging, and painting on and off the computer. This is labor of love she hopes will help raise money and awareness for sex-positivity and sexual education resources.
