Pansexual Erotica Coloring Book

About

The coloring book aims to celebrate sexual expression, positivity, and promote public discussion and concern with sexual health.
Yaycake is an artist who enjoys drawing, collaging, and painting on and off the computer. The coloring book is a labor of love she hopes will help raise money and awareness for sex-positive sexual education resources.
